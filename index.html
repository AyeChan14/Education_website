Meet Our Qulified Teachers

Our Math Teacher

  • Holds a Master's degree in Mathematics Education.
  • Possesses over 10 years of teaching experience in high school and college settings.
  • Has a track record of improving student math scores by implementing innovative teaching methods and personalized learning approaches.
  • Participates regularly in professional development workshops and conferences to stay updated with the latest teaching techniques and curriculum advancements.

Our English Teacher

  • Specializes in teaching English as a Second Language (ESL) and has experience working with diverse student populations.
  • Utilizes a variety of teaching methods, including literature analysis, writing workshops, and multimedia resources, to cater to different learning styles.
  • Actively involved in extracurricular activities such as literary clubs and drama productions to foster a love for language and literature among students.

Our Music Teacher

  • Holds a Bachelor's degree in Music Education or Fine Arts.
  • Proficient in multiple musical instruments and/or various art mediums.
  • Has taught music and arts in both public and private school settings, with a focus on fostering creativity and self-expression.
  • Organizes regular performances and art exhibitions to showcase students' talents and achievements, creating a supportive and encouraging learning environment.
